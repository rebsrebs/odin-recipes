Ingredients

  • 1 can of beans
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 clove garlic sliced thin
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Rinse beans in strainer under tap water.
  2. Heat olive oil in sauce pan.
  3. Saut√© garlic until fragrant, around thirty seconds.
  4. Use a spatula to remove garlic slices and discard, keeping oil in pan.
  5. Add beans and water to oil in pan.
  6. Cook on low until water is absorbed.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
