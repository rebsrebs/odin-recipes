Oatmeal

Description

lalalala

lalalal

Ingredients

  • old fashioned rolled oats
  • water
  • salt

Steps

  1. bring water to a boil
  2. reduce heat to simmer
  3. add pinch of salt
  4. add oatmeal
  5. stir frequently
  6. after 5 minutes or when all water is absorbed, remove oatmeal from heat and serve.
